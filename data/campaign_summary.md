# Campaign Performance Summary 2024-2025

## Review of "Dark Orbit" vs "Last Kingdom"
When we compare Dark Orbit vs Last Kingdom, the differences in audience retention are stark. Dark Orbit launched with a massive TV ad spend ($800k) but suffered a severe drop-off after episode 3, resulting in only a 40% ROI.

On the other hand, Last Kingdom utilized a community-first social media approach ($300k spend) and yielded an incredible 250% ROI due to strong word-of-mouth. 

## Audience Segments
The most engaged audience segment consists of Premium subscribers aged 25-34 in North America and Canada. They binge-watched Sci-Fi bundles at double the rate of Basic tier subscribers.

## Next Steps
We must move away from expensive TV ad spots for niche genres and lean heavily into social media influencer partnerships, which have repeatedly shown higher ROI for our entertainment catalog.
